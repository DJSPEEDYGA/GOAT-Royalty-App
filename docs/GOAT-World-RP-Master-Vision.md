### 4. 🎯 The Vetting Process

**Phase 1: Discovery**
- Player finds GOAT World RP
- Joins the FiveM server
- Creates character

**Phase 2: Observation**
- AI agents interact and remember
- Player behavior is logged
- Red flags identified early
- Positive actions noted

**Phase 3: Trust Building**
- Player completes missions
- Helps other players
- Shows consistency over time
- Gets referrals from trusted members

**Phase 4: Invitation**
- Trusted players get invited to "inner circle"
- Access to GOAT Royalty App features
- Can interact with real data
- Becomes part of the family

**Trust Score Components:**
| Factor | Weight | Duration |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Playtime | 10% | 30+ hours |
| Missions Completed | 15% | 10+ missions |
| Player Interactions | 20% | Positive ratings |
| Referrals | 25% | From trusted members |
| Background Check | 30% | Manual review |
