Launch Steps

Game Tutorial
1

Add Cfx.re license key to server.cfg locally

Get a free key from keymaster.fivem.net and add it to your local server.cfg file.

sv_licenseKey "YOUR_CFX_LICENSE_KEY_HERE"
2

Windows: Run server-artifacts/FXServer.exe

Navigate to your server artifacts folder and execute the FXServer binary.

FXServer.exe +exec server-data/server.cfg
3

Open txAdmin

After first launch, txAdmin will print a PIN to your console. Open the panel to complete setup.

http://127.0.0.1:40120
4

Configure server in txAdmin wizard

Follow the txAdmin setup wizard to configure your server name, resources, and admin credentials.

Save your txAdmin credentials somewhere safe. The master PIN only appears once on first launch.
5

Connect in FiveM

Launch FiveM, press F8, and connect to your server.

connect localhost:30120
6

Invite the crew — build GOAT City

Share the server IP with the squad. BrickSquaD runs deep. Time to build the empire in Los Santos.
