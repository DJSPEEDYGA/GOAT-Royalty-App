💡

What You'll NOT Get (and Why)

The Spotify API does not expose:

  • ❌ Individual listener names / emails / phones
  • ❌ Who streamed your song (no listener list)
  • ❌ Listener IP addresses or devices

This is federal privacy law (GDPR, CCPA) — no platform can give this. The legal way to build your fan list is via Smart Links + opt-in capture — which is what we've built into the GOAT App.
